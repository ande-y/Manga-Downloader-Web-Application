Manga Downloader

Powered by MangaDex Api & Google Drive Api



1.

Input the manga name.

2.

Select the manga that can be found on MangaDex.

3.

Select the chapter in that manga.

4.

Authenticate your Google Drive Account for the download.

5.

Wait for the pages to be uploaded to your Google Drive.
Please be patient. The more pages there are in a the chapter, the long the wait will be.

6.

You'll automatically be sent to the Drive Folder with all the pages when the download completes.





* Please note that many mainstream mangas such as『One Piece』are licensed heavily by their publishers.

* Though MangaDex may provide methods to access those manga on their website, they may not necessarily have the manga in their database for us to download due to copyright & distribution enforcement.

* Series that exist on MangaDex but are not downloadable will return a 404.
